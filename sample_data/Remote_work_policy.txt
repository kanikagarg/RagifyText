**Remote Work Policy Document**

---

**Purpose**: To define the framework under which employees at ACompany may work remotely, ensuring flexibility, productivity, and alignment with business goals.

**Scope**: This policy applies to all eligible full-time and part-time employees of ACompany. It does not apply to contractors unless otherwise specified in their contract.

---

### 1. Eligibility Criteria

* Employees must have completed their 90-day probation period.
* Employees must have demonstrated the ability to work independently and meet performance expectations.
* Roles that are client-facing, involve physical infrastructure, or require on-site presence may be excluded unless otherwise approved.

---

### 2. Remote Work Types

* **Hybrid**: A mix of remote and on-site days (e.g., 2–3 days remote/week).
* **Fully Remote**: Employees working 100% remotely from an approved location.

Requests for remote status should be submitted to HR and approved by the department head.

---

### 3. Work Hours and Availability

* Maintain core working hours: **9:00 AM to 6:00 PM** local time.
* Be available during team meetings, stand-ups, and client calls.
* Update daily status in the team communication tool (e.g., Slack, Teams).

---

### 4. Communication Guidelines

* Use company-approved communication tools (Slack, Zoom, Gmail).
* Video must be enabled during scheduled video meetings unless exceptions are granted.
* Respond to internal messages/emails within 1 business day.

---

### 5. Equipment and IT Support

* Eligible employees will be issued:

  * ACompany laptop
  * Mouse, headset (if needed)
  * VPN and security tools
* Internet stipend of **$50/month** reimbursed via the expense policy.
* Report technical issues immediately to: `it-support@acompany.com`

---

### 6. Security and Confidentiality

* All remote work must adhere to ACompany’s IT and data protection policies.
* Use VPN at all times while accessing company systems.
* Devices must be locked when not in use.
* No use of public/shared computers for company work.

---

### 7. Workspace Requirements

* Employees must have a quiet, secure, and ergonomically sound environment.
* Internet speed must support video calls and system access without lag.

---

### 8. Performance Monitoring

* Managers will evaluate remote work performance based on:

  * Output and quality of deliverables
  * Timeliness
  * Communication
  * Collaboration
* Poor performance may lead to remote work privileges being revoked.

---

### 9. Travel and Work-from-Anywhere (WFA)

* Employees may request to work temporarily from another city or country.
* Approval required from the manager and HR.
* Must confirm compliance with data residency, labor, and tax regulations.

---

### 10. Policy Violations

* Non-compliance may result in:

  * Warning
  * Revocation of remote privileges
  * Disciplinary action per HR policies

---

**Contact Information**:

* HR Team: `hr@acompany.com`
* IT Support: `it-support@acompany.com`
