**Holiday Policy Document**

---

**Purpose**: To outline the official company holidays recognized by ACompany and provide guidelines for floating and optional holidays.

**Scope**: This policy applies to all employees of ACompany, regardless of work location or department.

---

### Floating Holidays

* Employees are entitled to **2 floating holidays** per year.
* These may be used for religious, cultural, or regional observances.
* Must be requested via the HR portal and approved by the manager at least **5 business days** in advance.

---

### Optional Observances

* ACompany recognizes that employees may wish to observe additional holidays or regional events.
* Employees may request **unpaid time off** or use available **Casual Leave** for such occasions.

---

### Weekend Holiday Adjustments

* If a fixed holiday falls on a **Saturday**, it will be observed on the **preceding Friday**.
* If it falls on a **Sunday**, it will be observed on the **following Monday**.

---

### Holiday Pay

* Employees required to work on a fixed holiday due to operational needs will receive **1.5x regular pay** or a **compensatory day off**, subject to managerial approval.

---

### Blackout Periods

* Teams involved in mission-critical functions may be required to work on holidays.
* Such periods will be communicated by department heads well in advance.

---

### Policy Abuse

* Misuse or misrepresentation of holiday leave may result in a formal HR review and potential disciplinary action.
