Leave Policy

**Purpose**: To establish a structured and fair leave system that supports employee well-being, ensures business continuity, and complies with applicable labor laws.

**Scope**: This policy applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees of the company unless otherwise stated in their employment agreement.

**Types of Leave**:

1. **Annual Leave (Paid Time Off)**

   * **Entitlement**: 20 working days per year, accrued monthly (1.67 days/month).
   * **Eligibility**: Full-time employees after successful completion of a 3-month probation.
   * **Carry Forward**: Up to 5 days may be carried over to the following calendar year. Unused leave beyond that is forfeited.
   * **Encashment**: No leave encashment allowed unless on final settlement at separation.

2. **Sick Leave**

   * **Entitlement**: 10 working days per year.
   * **Usage**: For personal illness, injury, or medical appointments.
   * **Proof Required**: Medical certificate for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.
   * **Unutilized Sick Leave**: Not carried forward or encashed.

3. **Parental Leave**

   * **Maternity Leave**: 12 weeks (paid), extendable by 4 weeks (unpaid).
   * **Paternity Leave**: 2 weeks (paid), taken within 3 months of child's birth.
   * **Adoption Leave**: 8 weeks for primary caregiver of adopted child below 5 years.

4. **Bereavement Leave**

   * **Entitlement**: 3 working days per occurrence.
   * **Applicable For**: Death of immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling, grandparent).

5. **Casual Leave / Personal Leave**

   * **Entitlement**: 5 days per calendar year.
   * **Use**: For urgent personal matters not covered under other leave types.

6. **Unpaid Leave (Leave of Absence)**

   * **Eligibility**: Available upon exhaustion of paid leave, subject to manager and HR approval.
   * **Duration**: Up to 30 days, case-by-case review beyond that.

**Leave Request Procedure**:

* Apply through the HR portal at least **2 weeks in advance** (except emergencies).
* Direct manager and HR approval required.
* Emergency/sick leave must be notified within the same working day.

**Leave Calendar Blackout Periods**:

* Certain roles may be restricted from taking leave during peak operational periods (e.g., end-of-quarter, product launch).
* Teams will be notified in advance of such blackout dates.

**Abuse of Leave Policy**:

* Pattern of frequent absenteeism or misuse may result in disciplinary action.
* HR will conduct leave audits and flag irregularities.

**Separation Policy**:

* Outstanding leave balance paid out only for Annual Leave upon resignation/termination.
* Employee must clear handovers before final leave approval.
